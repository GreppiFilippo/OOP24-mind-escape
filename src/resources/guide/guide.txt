Welcome to Mind Escape!

In this thrilling text-based adventure, your goal is to solve challenges to progress through different rooms. Each room hides secrets and enigmas that you must decipher to move forward.

Controls:
W - Move forward
A - Move left
S - Move backward
D - Move right
E - Interact with objects
I - Open inventory
I (again) - Close inventory

Use your logic, creativity, and observation skills to find clues and solve the mysteries. Every detail matters, so explore carefully!

Good luck, and enjoy your adventure in Mind Escape!
